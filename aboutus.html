about us

the name

Coffee is believed to have originated in the 15th century from the Kaffa region in Ethiopia, Africa.

For about 500 years, the Kaffa region was inhabited by the Oromo people. It it belived that the Oromo people were the first coffee-drinkers around the world.

Due to coffee's sensational aroma, the name oroma was formed. A distinctive name, with tribute to the orginiators of the coffee we all enjoy.

the goal

Our goal is to fill the gap in the coffee and tea knowledge space.

There are many ways to enjoy your favourite coffee or tea beverage. Oroma wants to make sure you can enjoy your favourite high quality drinks at home, without breaking the bank.

Informative and affordable!
