what you need

kettle (or boiling water)

ground coffee (medium setting)

pour over (funnel)

coffee filter

timer

01

Boil the water, making sure to have enough for your desired amount of coffee.

02

Prepare filter on pour over with cup below.

03

Wet the filter with water and empty the excess water into the sink.

04

Add the ground coffee (X tbsp coffee per X oz or X mL water) to the filter and replace the pour over with cup below.

05

Slowly pour the hot water on top of the grinds, only enough to wet all of the grinds.

06

Once the grinds are all wet, stop pouring the water and wait about a minute. This is called blooming and allows for the coffee to begin releasing its flavor.

07

Pour the remaining hot water into the filter and wait for the coffee to brew into the cup.

08

Discard the filter and serve immediately!
